More about kandadhar beauty

Rudana Village, a small village in Sirmaur district of Himachal Pradesh, is situated in the mountains. Rudana village is far away from noise. The forests here surrounded by cedar mountains are very attractive. Kandadhaar is situated at a quiet and elevated place In the summer season, the calm sunshine and cool and calm winds give it a different peaceful atmosphere and beauty. Kandadhaar is one of the most beautiful places of Rudana village which cannot be considered less than the seventh wonder among the 14 villages of Aanj Bhoj. Every type of facility will be provided to you here. This village is surrounded by lush green and clean environment. There are cold and snowy mountains around Ruthana village like Shilai ,Churidar and Chakrata and Jaunsar mountains of Uttarakhand.The natural beauty here will fascinate you. Himachal Pradesh is crowded with tourists during the summer season, so if you want to spend a relaxing moment, then you can come to Rudana village, a small village in Sirmaur district of Himachal Pradesh
