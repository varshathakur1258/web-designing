More about Paragliding Services

Paragliding is the recreational and competitive adventure sport of flying paraglide: lightweight, free- flying, foot-launched glider aircraft with no rigid primary structure. The pilot sits in a harness suspended below a fabric wing. Among the health benefits of paragliding, you will also help to build a strong core, which helps with balance, posture, and stability.

*the location of paraglidingThe location of paragliding has been kept on a high mountain from where it can help in flying. We will provide you paragliding services which is Paragliding canopy or wing, harness, spare parachute, helmet, radio, pair of gloves, pair of sunglasses and appropriate shoes.*paragliding safety What is the age limit for paragliding?
The minimum age required for paragliding is 12 years. The weight limit is 30 kg to 90 kg, if the weight is more than 90 to 110 kg, pilot fee will be Rs 500/- and will depend on the weather and fitness of the guests.
Your pilot will have full control of the wing at all times. It's also worth noting that there is no "jump" off the hill. Instead, you are gently lifted off the ground, so you don't feel your stomach drop.
