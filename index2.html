About-Kandadhar

More about our website

Kandadhar is a place in Distt. Sirmour in Anjbhoj village Rudana. If we talk about this place beauty is beyond your imagination but for the development of this place we are developing a tourism spot. Here from the past few years many important necessary things are being transport here but providing these necessary items is a very difficult work due to the fact this place is in mountain or we can say in hilly areas. First for the development many link road are develop for linking this place to the outside place and developing these link road is a very difficult work. Later on for tourist many developments like swimming pool , camping , tracking, paragliding, home stay etc. and also provide healthy village food related to our culture.

* The culture here itself is very good for us, the pahari nati and traditional musicial instruments present a beauty in themseleves and the pahari food here is also very good and healthy.

* The people here are very peaceful and kind hearted, they welcome the guests comming here with open hearts

.

The area here is blessed with a very peaceful and beautiful environment, noise and pollution free environment without any vehicles, pure air and a small village surrounded by lush green trees and big mountains all around.

Our dream is to make our village a developed tourist place so that people can interact with the culture of our village and admire the beauty of nature.
